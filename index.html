WHAT DOES THAT MEAN?

CLIMATE + CHANGE = CLIMATE CHANGE:

Climate is the weather conditions occurinf in an area in general or over a long period of time. For example, Many people love to go to Florida for Spring break because it has a tropical climate meaning that the weather is warm during the Spring season.

CHANGE: the act or instance of making of becoming different. For example, when you go through puberty your body changes as you become a young adult.

You put these two words together and BAM! you get CLIMATE CHANGE.

Climate Change is exactly what it sounds like, it's when there is a big difference in normal climate patterns over a long time.
